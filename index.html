Welcome to Ranippettai Engineering College

Education is the best key to success in life

Established in 1999, Ranippettai Engineering College is a premier self-financing institution under the Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Educational Trust. Our mission is to empower students with high-quality technical education that fosters academic excellence and practical skills.

Approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, we offer an industry-ready curriculum, supported by experienced faculty, modern labs, and an inspiring campus environment.
